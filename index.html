A psychological horror experience where reality and madness collide

What is Sanity Escape?

A journalist awakens in a nightmarish asylum with no memory of how he arrived. Navigate shifting corridors, face disturbing patients, and uncover dark secrets while questioning what's real and what's madness.

Game Details

Genre: Psychological Horror
Platform: PC, Playstation, and Xbox
Target Audience: Ages 16+, Horror Game Enthusiasts
Development Status: In Development

Key Features

  • Immersive psychological horror atmosphere
  • Dynamic asylum that shifts and changes
  • Mysterious AI entity companion/antagonist
  • Environmental storytelling and puzzles
  • Multiple endings based on player choices
  • Unique "gaze" mechanic to stop enemies
